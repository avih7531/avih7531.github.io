Our Background

Rejewvenate is a Jewish home for students on campus, regardless of background or affiliation.

We are creating a space for Jews to connect with each other and themselves. A place to learn, a place you can come and question or have a chat with the Rabbi, Rebbetzin, and friends.

We have Shabbat dinners, interactive holiday events, cooking, culture, and much more.

- The Rejewvenate Team
