Our Mission

At Rejewvenate, our mission is to create a welcoming, vibrant Jewish home for students on campus, regardless of background or affiliation. We believe that every Jewish student deserves a space where they can explore their heritage, connect with peers, and grow spiritually in a supportive environment.

We strive to be a bridge between traditional Jewish values and contemporary student life, offering meaningful experiences that resonate with today's young adults while honoring our rich cultural heritage.

Blaze your own path. You can be sure that it has not been sullied by others.

- Rabbi Menachem Mendel Schneerson

What We Offer

Shabbat Experiences

Weekly Shabbat dinners that bring students together for delicious meals, inspiring conversations, and the warmth of Jewish tradition.

Holiday Celebrations

Vibrant, interactive celebrations of Jewish holidays throughout the year, making ancient traditions accessible and meaningful for today's students.

Learning Opportunities

Classes, discussions, and one-on-one learning opportunities that allow students to explore Jewish wisdom at their own pace.

Community Events

Social gatherings, cooking workshops, cultural experiences, and other events that build lasting friendships and connections.

Personal Guidance

Support and guidance from our dedicated team, offering a listening ear and thoughtful perspective on life's questions and challenges.
