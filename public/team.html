Our Team

Meet the passionate people behind Rejewvenate

The Heart of Our Community

At Rejewvenate, our team is committed to creating a warm, vibrant Jewish community for students on campus. Each member brings their own unique perspective and talents to our shared mission.

Rabbi Yisrolik Kievman

Co-Founder

Rabbi Yisrolik is a beloved and dedicated leader, proudly serving as the co-founder and rabbi of Rejewvenate and Rejewvenate Grads, where he serves the Downtown NYC Jewish community with warmth, vision, and deep commitment. Growing up in Liverpool, England, Rabbi Yisrolik was raised in a home that ran a vibrant community Chabad, where hospitality, communal responsibility, and love for every Jew were a way of life. From a young age, he absorbed what it means to build and sustain a Jewish community rooted in Torah, chesed, and genuine care.


That upbringing deeply shaped his rabbinic approach today. Rabbi Yisrolik is known for his authenticity, accessibility, and passion for helping others grow in their Yiddishkeit. Through meaningful relationships, inspiring Torah learning, and an open-door home, he has helped create Rejewvenate as a place where students and young professionals feel empowered, connected, and truly at home.

Rebbetzin Liba Kievman

Co-Founder

Liba, Rebbetzin and co-founder of Rejewvenate and Rejewvenate Grads, embraces her role as a leader, mentor, and nurturer with great devotion. Having grown up in Downtown Manhattan surrounded by college students, she developed a deep appreciation for Chabad life from a young age. Liba loves cooking, hosting, and being a motherly presence to all who walk through the doors of the Rejewvenate home. She feels deeply honored to continue the Chabad legacy and to create a space filled with warmth, growth, and connection.
